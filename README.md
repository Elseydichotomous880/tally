# 📊 tally - Track every AI subscription cost easily

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Elseydichotomous880/tally/releases)

Tally helps you manage your AI tool subscriptions. It acts as a monitor for your accounts. You can track rate limits for services like Claude and Codex. It displays your usage statistics in your computer menu bar. The software ensures you stay within your limits. It switches accounts to give you the most room for your work.

## 📥 How to Install Tally

1. Visit the [releases page](https://github.com/Elseydichotomous880/tally/releases) to download the latest version.
2. Look for the file ending in .dmg for your macOS system.
3. Open the downloaded file once the download finishes.
4. Drag the Tally application icon into your Applications folder.
5. Open your Applications folder and double-click Tally to start the program.

## ⚙️ Setting Up Your Accounts

Tally requests access to your AI provider accounts to check your usage. You need your login tokens or API keys from services like Anthropic or OpenAI.

1. Open Tally from your menu bar. 
2. Click the gear icon to open settings.
3. Select the AI provider you currently use.
4. Paste the requested API key into the input field.
5. Click Save. 
6. Repeat these steps for every account you want to monitor.

The app secures your credentials on your device. It communicates only with the official servers of the AI providers. It does not share your data with third parties.

## 🖥️ Using the Menu Bar

Tally sits in your menu bar at the top of your screen. Click the small icon to see the dashboard. The window shows your current usage for each provider. Colors indicate your status. Green means you have plenty of quota. Yellow warns that you approach your limit. Red signals that you hit your limit and should switch accounts.

The app includes a command line feature. This tool monitors your headroom in the background. It automatically shifts your session to the account with the most capacity remaining. You prevent interruptions in your workflow with this feature.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Problems

New users sometimes face issues during the initial setup. Follow these tips to resolve simple errors.

* **The icon does not appear:** Restart your computer. Check your system settings to ensure Tally has permission to run at startup.
* **Usage data stays at zero:** Your API keys might be incorrect. Open settings again. Check that every character in the key matches your portal account exactly.
* **The app closes unexpectedly:** Ensure your macOS version is up to date. Tally requires the latest security updates to function properly.
* **Rate limits still occur:** Some providers define very strict limits. Tally monitors these but cannot force the provider to increase your cap.

## 📋 System Requirements

Tally requires macOS version 12.0 or newer. It needs an active internet connection to refresh your usage data. You must have valid subscriptions for the AI services you track. The app consumes very little memory. It uses less than one percent of your processor power while active.

## 📝 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Tally save my personal chat history?**
No. Tally only reads your subscription status and rate limit headers. It does not access your prompts or your generated content.

**Can I use Tally with more than three accounts?**
Yes. You can add as many accounts as you possess. The menu bar expands to fit the list.

**Why does the icon change color?**
The color identifies your health status relative to your monthly or hourly allowance. It provides a visual nudge to manage your usage without searching through browser tabs.

**What is the command line tool?**
Advanced users can trigger the CLI from a terminal. It allows for advanced automation scripts. You do not need to use this unless you want to integrate Tally into your own custom software tools.

## 🔒 Your Privacy

Tally maintains a strict privacy policy. All data remains on your local machine. We do not track your clicks or your habits. The software performs all calculations locally. Your API keys are encrypted using your system keychain. No person at our organization can access your account details.

## 📈 Maintaining Your Status

Keep your keys updated. If you reset your password on an AI website, you likely generate a new key. Update this key in the Tally settings menu immediately to keep the monitor active. Set a reminder in your calendar once a month to check that all keys remain valid. This ensures you never face a stop in your productivity during a busy workday.
